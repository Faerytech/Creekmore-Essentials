What We Use

Every ingredient earns its place

Our goat milk soap base is rich in vitamins and naturally gentle on skin. We choose every oil for a reason — cocoa butter for softness, castor oil for lather, almond oil to nourish. No fillers. No shortcuts.

  • Goat Milk
  • Olive Oil
  • Coconut Oil
  • Cocoa Butter
  • Almond Oil
  • Castor Oil
  • Vitamin E Oil
  • Pure Fragrance

We never list lye — because there's none left. The saponification process transforms it completely, leaving only rich, nourishing soap.
