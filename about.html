The Story

Made by hand.
Made with intention.

Creekmore Essentials was born from a simple belief: what you put on your skin matters just as much as what you put in your body. Every product is handcrafted in small batches by Deanna Martino in Hattiesburg, Mississippi.

Our Philosophy

Why goat milk?

There's something special about goat milk soap that you feel the first time you use it. Rich, creamy, gentle — it doesn't strip your skin the way commercial soaps do. That difference is what inspired Creekmore Essentials.

Goat milk is packed with vitamins A, B, C, D, E, and K, plus lactic acid that naturally exfoliates and brightens. When combined with nourishing oils like cocoa butter, almond oil, and olive oil, the result is a bar of soap that actually cares for your skin.

Everything we make starts with that same commitment to quality. Our body butters are whipped from real tallow and shea. Our salves use beeswax and botanical oils. Our beard care is formulated for real results. No shortcuts. No fillers. No compromises.

Creekmore Essentials products are available online and at Artecera in Hattiesburg. When you shop with us, you're supporting a small, one-woman operation that genuinely loves what it makes.
